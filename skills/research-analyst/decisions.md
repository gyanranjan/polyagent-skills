# Research Analyst — Methodological Decisions

_Record decisions about research methodology, source quality standards, and when to conclude research._

## Decision Log

### 2026-03-06 — Evidence Categorization Standard
**Decision**: Use four-tier evidence tagging: FACT / INTERPRETATION / ASSUMPTION / UNRESOLVED.
**Rationale**: Prevents conflation of different confidence levels in research output, which leads to poor downstream decisions.
**Applied to**: All research reports produced by this role.
