# Ideator — Selected Ideas for Development

_Record ideas that have been selected for further development, who selected them, and which role is taking them forward. This is not a decision log in the strategic sense — it is a handoff record tracking which ideas graduated out of ideation._

## Handoff Log

_No ideas selected for development yet. Entries will be added after ideation sessions produce top picks that are handed off to entrepreneur, product-manager, or other roles._

---

## Entry Format (for future use)
```
### [Idea Name] — [Date Selected]
**From session**: [Topic / challenge that spawned this idea]
**One-line description**: [What the idea is]
**Selected by**: [Role or user who approved escalation]
**Handed to**: [Role taking this idea forward]
**Why selected**: [Rationale — what made this stand out]
**Status**: [In development / Validated / Dropped]
```
