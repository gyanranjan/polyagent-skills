# Decisions — historian-knowledge-curator

This file is a meta-record: decisions made by this role about what to preserve, how to organize knowledge, and how to prioritize curation work. It does not duplicate the decision records this role captures on behalf of other roles (those are stored in the shared project files or provided back to the originating roles).

---

## Format

Each record includes:
- **ID**: Unique identifier for cross-referencing
- **Date**: When the meta-decision was made
- **Question**: The curation or organizational decision being made
- **Decision**: What was decided
- **Rationale**: Why
- **Status**: Active / Superseded
