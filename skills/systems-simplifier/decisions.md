# Decisions — systems-simplifier

A record of simplification recommendations made by this role: which were approved, which were rejected, and why. This file is the authoritative log of simplification outcomes.

---

## Format

Each record includes:
- **ID**: Unique identifier for cross-referencing
- **Date**: When the decision was made
- **Target**: What was being simplified
- **Recommendation**: What was proposed
- **Status**: Approved / Rejected / Deferred
- **Rationale**: Why the decision went the way it did
- **Decided by**: Which role or person made the final call
