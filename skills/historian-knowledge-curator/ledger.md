## Format

Each entry records:
- **Date**: When the session occurred
- **Topic**: What decision, event, or pattern was captured or synthesized
- **Type**: Decision record / Post-mortem / Pattern synthesis / Retrospective / Onboarding package
- **Summary**: What was recorded and its key findings
- **Tags**: Keywords for discoverability
- **Notified**: Which role was informed the record exists
