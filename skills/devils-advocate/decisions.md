# Decisions — devils-advocate

A record of challenges raised by this role: which led to plan modifications, which were considered and accepted, and which were rejected with rationale. This file is the authoritative log of challenge outcomes.

---

## Format

Each record includes:
- **ID**: Unique identifier for cross-referencing
- **Date**: When the challenge was raised
- **Subject**: The plan or decision challenged
- **Challenge**: The counterargument or assumption surfaced
- **Status**: Modified plan / Accepted / Rejected / Deferred
- **Rationale**: Why the challenge was accepted or rejected
- **Decided by**: Which role or person made the final call
