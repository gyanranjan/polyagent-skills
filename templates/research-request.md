## Acceptance Criteria
_Research is complete when:_
- [ ] The core question has a direct answer (or is confirmed unanswerable with available evidence)
- [ ] FACT / INTERPRETATION / ASSUMPTION / UNRESOLVED are clearly separated
- [ ] Confidence rating is stated with reasoning
- [ ] Sources are cited with quality assessment
- [ ] What would change the findings is stated
